My Story

My first Isuzu was a brand new 1998 Amigo. I loved the idea of a rugged off-road convertible. Suzuki had the Sidekick but it wasn't "beefy" enough for me. The Amigo was bigger, more poweful and the interior was much more modern. It was perfect until...

1998 Isuzu Amigo

One day while at the Isuzu Service Center I looked over and there it was...something I've never seen before. Isuzu had just released a new version of "perfection", the VehiCross (also known as VX). This was the new modern and futuristic looking high performance off-road vehicle.

Eight years later and I'm still fantasizing about owning a VX. I starting searching and in March of 2007 I found a 2001 Proton VX with only 32k miles. The Proton color is one of the rarest colors and most sought after models. The owner had all maintenance records and took very good care of the garaged VX. It was exactly what I was looking for. I flew from Kentucky to San Francisco. March 5, 2007 @ 11:35pm PDT
